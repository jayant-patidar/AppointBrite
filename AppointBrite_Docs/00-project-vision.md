# Document 00 — Project Vision & Business Model (v1.0)
**Project Name:** AppointBrite

## 1. Executive Summary

AppointBrite is a two-sided marketplace platform that enables users to discover, compare, and book real-time appointments with local service-based businesses.

It modernizes how people interact with local services by replacing fragmented booking methods (phone calls, DMs, manual calendars, walk-ins) with a unified, intelligent, real-time scheduling system.

The platform connects:
- **Customers** looking for services (e.g., salons, restaurants, clinics, gyms)
- **Businesses** offering appointment-based services
- **Administrators** managing platform integrity, trust, and operations

AppointBrite aims to become the “Uber Eats for appointments and reservations”, but extended across all appointment-driven industries.

## 2. Vision, Mission & Core Values

### Vision
To become the global operating system for booking local services in real time.

### Mission
To simplify how people discover and book services while helping local businesses fully digitize and optimize their appointment operations.

### Core Values
- **Simplicity:** Booking should take under 30 seconds
- **Transparency:** Real-time availability, pricing, and reviews
- **Trust:** Verified businesses and authentic user feedback
- **Efficiency:** Reduce idle time for businesses, reduce friction for users
- **Accessibility:** Any business, any size, can join and grow

## 3. The Problem We’re Solving

### For Customers:
- Difficult to find available slots instantly
- Need to call or message multiple businesses
- No unified system for comparing options
- Missed appointments and poor reminders
- Lack of trust in availability and pricing accuracy

### For Businesses:
- Manual scheduling systems (phone, WhatsApp, paper calendars)
- High no-show rates
- Inefficient calendar utilization
- Difficulty acquiring new customers
- No centralized customer management system

## 4. The Solution

AppointBrite provides a unified platform where:

**Customers can:**
- Search nearby services instantly
- See real-time availability
- Book appointments instantly
- Pay online (optional)
- Receive reminders and updates
- Leave reviews and ratings

**Businesses can:**
- Manage digital calendars
- Accept/reject bookings
- Set availability rules
- Reduce no-shows via reminders & deposits
- Gain new customers via marketplace discovery
- Track analytics and performance

## 5. Market Opportunity

Appointment-based services represent a massive global market:
- Beauty & wellness (salons, spas)
- Healthcare (clinics, dentists, therapists)
- Fitness (gyms, trainers, yoga studios)
- Food & hospitality (restaurants, events)
- Professional services (lawyers, consultants, tutors)

This is a multi-trillion-dollar fragmented service economy that is still heavily offline or semi-digital.
The opportunity is to unify this into a single booking infrastructure layer.

## 9. SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- Unified cross-industry platform
- High scalability potential
- Strong marketplace network effects

### Weaknesses
- Cold start problem (need supply + demand balance)
- High complexity of multiple industries

### Opportunities
- Global shift toward digital booking
- SMB digitization trend
- AI-assisted scheduling optimization

### Threats
- Existing vertical SaaS platforms
- Slow adoption by traditional businesses

## 10. Success Metrics

- Number of active businesses
- Number of bookings per day
- Booking completion rate
- Customer retention rate
- Business churn rate
- Average booking value
