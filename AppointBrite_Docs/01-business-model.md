# Document 01 — Business Model (v1.0)
**Project Name:** AppointBrite

## 1. Business Model Overview

AppointBrite operates as a two-sided marketplace, acting as the intermediary between service-seeking customers and service-providing businesses.

The platform provides a centralized infrastructure for discovery, scheduling, and payment. Value is generated by bringing demand (customers) to supply (businesses) and reducing friction in the booking process.

## 2. Revenue Streams

### Core Marketplace Commission
- **Percentage Fee:** A small percentage (e.g., 2-5%) on every booking completed and paid through the platform.
- **Fixed Transaction Fee:** A small fixed fee for processing payments, passed onto the business or customer.

### Business Subscription Plans (SaaS)
- **Free Tier (Basic Listing):** Allows basic discovery, limited monthly bookings, and manual calendar management. Ideal for sole practitioners or new businesses.
- **Pro Tier (Automation & Analytics):** Paid monthly subscription offering automated SMS/email reminders, CRM tools, advanced analytics, and integrated payments.
- **Enterprise Tier (Multi-Location):** Premium tier for franchise chains or multi-location businesses, offering centralized dashboards, advanced reporting, staff management, and custom APIs.

### Premium Placement (Advertising)
- **Featured Listings:** Businesses can pay for a visibility boost in search results for their specific category or geographical area.
- **Promotional Banners:** Highlighted discounts or seasonal offers displayed on the app’s homepage.

## 3. Marketplace Strategy & Rollout

### Phase 1: Liquidity & Vertical Focus
- **Strategy:** Solve the "cold start" problem by focusing on one specific vertical (e.g., Hair Salons & Spas) in a single geographical area (e.g., one major city).
- **Goal:** Ensure supply density (enough businesses to offer real choice) and demand liquidity (enough customers to bring value to those businesses).

### Phase 2: Horizontal Expansion
- **Strategy:** Once the core booking engine is proven, expand horizontally into adjacent categories (Restaurants, Fitness, Medical Clinics).
- **Goal:** Capitalize on the existing customer base by offering them more reasons to open the app.

### Phase 3: Global & Enterprise Scaling
- **Strategy:** Expand geographically to new cities/countries. Onboard large enterprise chains with custom integrations.
- **Goal:** Establish market dominance as the universal booking layer.

## 4. Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors
- **Beauty & Wellness:** Booksy, Fresha, Vagaro, Mindbody.
- **Restaurants:** OpenTable, Resy, Yelp Reservations.
- **Healthcare/Professional:** Zocdoc, Calendly.

### The Gap (Our Advantage)
Most existing platforms are heavily siloed into specific verticals. A user needs 3-4 different apps to book a haircut, a dinner reservation, and a dental appointment. 

**AppointBrite differentiates by being a unified, multi-industry marketplace.** We reduce app fatigue for the consumer while providing businesses access to a broader, cross-pollinated user base.

## 5. Risk Mitigation Strategies

- **No-Show Prevention:** Implement automated reminders, mandatory deposits, and a user rating/penalty system to protect business revenue.
- **Disintermediation (Going Off-Platform):** Offer loyalty programs and exclusive discounts that incentivize customers to keep booking through AppointBrite rather than calling the business directly.
- **Quality Control:** Implement a strict review verification system and onboard high-quality "anchor" businesses early to establish platform trust.
